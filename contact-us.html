Some common questions
were often asked

How are shipping prices calculated?

Shipping prices depend on location, number of items, shipping method, and item type.

What is the return policy?

You can return your merchandise to our warehouse up to 90 days from the item's ship date. Returns must be unworn/unused with original tags and labels.

WHow do I track my order after it ships?

Once your order ships from our facility, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information, or you can visit Track My Order.

What happens if my order is canceled?

When we are unable to process an order, we will automatically cancel and refund your item. You will receive an email confirmation notifying you of this change. Please note that it can take up to 2-7 business days for the refund to reflect on your account statement.

Do you offer customized items?

Yes, we offer a wide selection of customized items for some of your favorite teams. However, please note that all sales are final, customized items cannot be modified or canceled once purchased, and they cannot be returned or refunded.

Do you have any coupons, promotions, or discounts?

Simply apply the provided code at the final step of checkout to see the discount deducted automatically from your cart total. Please note that only one promotion can be used per order, and certain brands or merchandise may be excluded from specific promotions.
